Mon Marché

Home delivery of fresh
fruit and vegetables

The challenge

Online food retailers have been gathering momentum for a long time, and COVID-19 has greatly contributed to the flourishing of catering and online product delivery services. Our team at Cuberto got to participate in the creation of a mobile application design concept for a big retailer in France. Our main goal was to show off the abundance of products. Then we had to meticulously consider the case of ordering, and its payment and delivery components. Throughout our collaboration, we offered several style options, successfully worked through the ordering case, and put together an interactive prototype for further testing.

We moved the main functions to the lower tab bar to make it easy to use with one hand.
Adding products makes the cart appear and you can check the total with one gesture.
